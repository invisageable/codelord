# Search

Find text across your entire project or within the current file.

![Search](assets/how-to/006-search.gif)

**How:** Press `Cmd+F` for in-file search, or `Cmd+Shift+F` for project-wide search. Type your query. Use `Enter` / `Shift+Enter` to jump between matches.

> *Tip: You can also say "find next" or "find previous" with voice control.*
