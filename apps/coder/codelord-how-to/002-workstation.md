# 002-workstation.

The workstation is your welcome screen. Three categories on the X-axis, items on the Y-axis.

![workstation](assets/how-to/002-workstation.gif)

**Categories:**

  - HACKERSPACE — *new file, open file, open folder.*
  - SiMULATiONS — *code editor, playground, copilord.*
  - SETTiNGS — *ui theme, keybinds, audio, Mmore.*

**How:** Arrow keys to browse, `Enter` to launch.

> *Tips: "Open Folder" is usually your first move — it loads a project into the explorer panel.*

![workstation-xmb](assets/how-to/002-workstation-xmb.gif)

You can toggle `Shift+Cmd+SPACE` to enter in voice mode and register your request — ask: `"Let's go to the code editor screen!"` then toggle back `Shift+Cmd+SPACE` to send your request to codelord. You can also toggle the voice control by using the `voice` button.

![workstation-voice-button](assets/how-to/002-workstation-voice-button.gif)
