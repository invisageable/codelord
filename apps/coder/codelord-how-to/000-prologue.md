# 000-prologue.

codelord is innovative but simple, like a gadget. This screen is the welcome screen workstation. You open files, folders, switch to another app (`code editor`, zo `playground`, etc.), access the settings, and so on. The gadget part is the voice control. You can talk to the codelord mentor to control your code editor with your voice. Like a game controller.

![First launch](assets/how-to/000-prologue.gif)

**What you're seeing:** The welcome screen loads. You open a
file. You start typing. That's it — you're coding.

> *Tip: Everything in codelord can be done with your voice, your keyboard, or both. Start with the keyboard, add voice when you're ready.*
